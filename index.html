HI THERE!


Who am I? My name is Adeboye Aladekoba or simply "Boye". I am a Computer Science Major at Alcorn State University, I like challenging myself to bring out the best of my abilities and enjoy learning everyday! I also enjoy holding leadership positions that enable me to have a positive impact on people around me, while also learning from them.

What is my background? Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. I have always had the passion for having a positive impact directly on people in my community which is what led me to study Computer Science. I am also the middle child out of 3, with 2 amazing sisters :)

Why Computer Science? I have always been that curuious kid wanting to know the "why". My interest came when I got my first laptop and I constantly bothered my parents for the usefulness of applications; the difference between the ctrl and alt key, and so on. This sticked around till it was time to pick a major and I could only think of Computer Science

What are my hobbies? Travelling! If I had the opportunity, I would travel all year long, literally, I love the feeling of exploring new places and seeing how people from different backgrounds or culture adapt. Diversity!

I also enjoy playing soccer and chess as well so if anyone is up for a challenge, please let me know!

What is my career goal? Although, I am still figuring out what my main career goal is, I know I want to be able to have a positive impact on the lives of people around the world, something I am really passionate about!
